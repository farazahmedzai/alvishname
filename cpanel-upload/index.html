Sindarin - The Grey-elvish Tongue

Sindarin, also known as Grey-elvish, was the language of the Eldar in Middle-earth. It evolved from Common Eldarin and became the most widely spoken Elvish language among the Elves who remained in Middle-earth. Sindarin names often reflect the natural world and the experiences of the Eldar.

Quenya - The High Elvish Language

Quenya, the "High Elvish" language, was the ancient tongue of the Eldar in Valinor. It was considered the most noble of the Elvish languages and was used for formal occasions, lore, and song. Quenya names often have a more archaic and ceremonial quality.

Pronunciation Guide

Elvish names follow specific pronunciation rules: 'c' is always hard like 'k', 'g' is always hard as in 'go', 'r' is rolled, and stress typically falls on the first syllable. The 'th' sound is pronounced as in 'think', and vowels are generally pure as in Latin.
